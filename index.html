AWESOME PONG GAME

Use Up and Down arrow keys to move your PAD

For two players use A,Z keys for Left Player and UP, Down Arrow keys for right player

Score:

Player One: 00 - Lives: 3

Round: 00

Player Two: 00 - Lives: 3

Press Space to pause and to start new round

Press Enter to start new game
