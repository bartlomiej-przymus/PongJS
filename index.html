AWESOME PONG GAME

Two player game no A.I. at the moment yet.

Only playable on laptop/desktop computers

Use A,Z keys for Left Player and Up, Down Arrow keys for right player.

Score:

Player One: 00 - Lives: 3

Round: 00

Player Two: 00 - Lives: 3

Press Space to pause and to start new round

Press Enter to start new game
